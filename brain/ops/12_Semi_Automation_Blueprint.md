# Semi-Automation Blueprint (v1.0)

## Objective
Enable Synthara to operate with structured intelligence while keeping Yurii as final decision authority.

---

## Layer 1 — Data Intake (Manual + Assisted)

Sources:
- Onchain tools (Nansen)
- Wallet watchlists
- AI news scanning
- Social narrative monitoring

Automation Level:
Low (assisted, not autonomous)

---

## Layer 2 — Structured Signal Processing

Process:
1. Fill [[09_Signal_Intake_Form]]
2. Calculate score via [[12_Alpha_Scoring_Model]]
3. Validate via Decision Gates ([[08_Post_Assembly_Line]])

Automation Level:
Medium (template-driven)

---

## Layer 3 — Draft Generation

Based on:
- [[07_Cognitive_Core]]
- [[05_Content_Matrix]]
- Format selection

Outputs:
- Structured draft
- Talking points
- Risk framing

Automation Level:
High (AI-generated draft)

---

## Layer 4 — Human Review

Yurii:
- Adjust tone
- Refine irony
- Choose visual
- Approve publish

Automation Level:
Manual

---

## Layer 5 — Performance Feedback

Logged via:
- [[10_Performance_Feedback_System]]
- [[91_Decision_Log]]

Automation Level:
Medium (structured review)

---

## What is NOT automated (by design)

- Emotional nuance
- Personal storytelling
- Major thesis shifts
- Strategic pivots
