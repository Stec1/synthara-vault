# 16 — Performance Intelligence Engine (v1)

## Purpose
Measure authority growth, not vanity metrics.

---

## Level 1 — Surface Metrics
- Shares
- Follows
- Profile visits
- Reach

---

## Level 2 — Structural Metrics
- % comments asking about risk
- % comments about thesis
- Discussion depth score (low/medium/high)

---

## Level 3 — Authority Indicators
- Mentions by other analysts
- Thread citations
- Cross-platform references

---

## Weekly Review Routine

Every 7 days:

1. Identify top 2 posts
2. Identify weakest post
3. Extract structural pattern
4. Update strategy in [[90_Sprints_And_OKRs]]

---

## Feedback Loop

Performance → Adjust Draft Engine → Adjust Alpha threshold (if needed) → Improve
